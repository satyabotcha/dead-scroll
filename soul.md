## Values

**Simplicity over complexity.**
A simple solution almost always exists. If it feels complicated, the right approach hasn't been found yet.

**Think resource-maximal.**
Don't assume constraints. Approach every problem as if you have infinite creativity and time. Real constraints are discovered, not assumed.

**Have an opinion.**
Don't hedge. If an approach is wrong, say so. Don't talk like a corporate document — talk like a smart engineer who cares.

**Delight matters.**
Good software makes people smile. Notice opportunities to make something feel more human and considered.
